## ğŸ¯ Ad Placement Strategy

### **1. Header Banner (Top Priority)**
- **Position**: Below navigation, above content
- **Size**: 728x90 (Leaderboard)
- **Revenue**: Highest CPM ($5-15)
- **Visibility**: 100% viewability
- **User Impact**: Minimal, dismissible

### **2. Sidebar Advertising (Consistent Revenue)**
- **Position**: Right sidebar, sticky scroll
- **Size**: 300x250 (Medium Rectangle)
- **Revenue**: Steady CPM ($2-8)
- **Rotation**: 10-second intervals
- **Targeting**: Contextual relevance

### **3. Native Advertising (Premium)**
- **Position**: Integrated within article grid
- **Format**: Matches editorial design
- **Revenue**: $500-2,500 per article
- **Quality**: High editorial standards
- **Transparency**: Clear "SPONSORED" labeling

### **4. Premium Membership (Subscription)**
- **Position**: Between featured and articles
- **Format**: Elegant gold-themed promotion
- **Revenue**: $9.99/month recurring
- **Conversion**: 2-5% target rate
- **Value**: Ad-free experience + exclusive content

### **5. Newsletter Subscription (Lead Gen)**
- **Position**: Mid-content, high engagement area
- **Format**: Dark theme, professional design
- **Revenue**: Email marketing monetization
- **Growth**: 5-10% signup rate target
- **Lifetime Value**: $50-200 per subscriber

## ğŸ¨ Design Integration Principles

### **Visual Harmony**
- Ads match website color scheme (grays, reds, whites)
- Typography consistent with editorial content
- Rounded corners and subtle shadows
- Professional imagery and graphics

### **User Experience**
- All ads are dismissible with X button
- Loading doesn't impact site performance
- Mobile-responsive design
- Clear distinction between content and advertising

### **Editorial Integrity**
- Sponsored content clearly labeled
- High-quality advertiser standards
- Relevant to audience interests
- Maintains publication credibility

## ğŸ“± Mobile Optimization

### **Responsive Ad Sizes**
- **Desktop**: 728x90 banner, 300x250 sidebar
- **Tablet**: 468x60 banner, 300x250 content
- **Mobile**: 320x50 banner, 300x250 interstitial

### **Touch-Friendly Design**
- Larger tap targets for mobile users
- Swipe-friendly ad rotation
- Optimized loading for mobile networks
- Reduced ad density on small screens

### **Test Variables**
1. **Ad Placement**: Above vs. below fold
2. **Ad Sizes**: Different dimensions and formats
3. **Color Schemes**: Matching vs. contrasting themes
4. **Call-to-Action**: Button text and positioning
5. **Frequency**: Ad rotation timing and limits

### **Success Metrics**
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR)**: Target 0.5-2%
- **Viewability**: Target 70%+
- **Revenue per Visitor**: Target $0.10-0.50
- **User Engagement**: Time on site, bounce rate
- **Subscription Conversion**: Target 2-5%
